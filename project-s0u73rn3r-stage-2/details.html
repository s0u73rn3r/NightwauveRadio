Background:

By using Spotify's Development API, it is capable of allowing users to sign into their accounts and play their music from the app. No more does one have to alt-tab into their spotify app, when it is just a tab in thier browser! Additionally, check out José Manuel Pérez's Listening Room he made with the Spotify Web API! With the ability for multiple users to add/remove to a playlist, it could make Nightwauve Radio into a fun party app!

Future Ideas:

As the background stated, i wish to fully implement this webpage into a working music player that links to Spotify. Some ideas that came to mind that people may want to intergrate this into are:

  • Workout Companion app-
     Have friends/family and maybe a community of people who share their workout music, and let people listen to a collaborative playlist
  • Music at a party-
     Let party-goers log into the app and able to change the music on the fly! Instead of have 1 person decide the fate of the music, everyone can have a say!

Why Synthwave?

Music has always been a huge part of life, I hardly do anything without it! I've always thought vaporwave/synthwave was a neat aeshtetic, so why not combine them? Plus I wanted to do something different from others, to stand out. I hope that the animinated aspects of it have acomplished that!
